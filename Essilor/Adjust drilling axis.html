Principle


This adjustment allows you to adjust the drilling axis in relation to the shape’s axis.


Reminders


You must check /adjust the drilling axis:

  • after a drilling spindle change,

  • after a shape axis position adjustment.


    Prerequisites


    Before setting the drilling axis, you must check:

    Mechanical aspects:

    • the condition of the drill bit and the drilling spindle (mechanical play)

    • optician’s working mode (lens material, type of cycle)

    • functioning of the play compensation system

    • retraction system locking (to avoid deviations if the module is not stable)

    • GMD module tilt lock.

      Via the HMI:

    • The output (Autotest on repeatability, error transmission via SIS)

    • The feelers (Autotest, Calibration if need be)

    • The centering of the shape of 22 and 18×14 mm


      • CR39 plastic flat lens (ref.: CX5055).

      • Posiblock and 22 mm accessories

      • Sliding stand

      • “45 mm circle” barcodes

Procedure: how to adjust the drilling axis


  1. Scan the “45×45 off-center drilling square” barcodes on the edging screen.


    > The shape is displayed in the working area.


  2. Decentralise the posiblock on a plastic lens to allow edging.


  3. Prepare the edging of the lens (right) in:


    • Flat-edge finish

    • With drilling

    • Without polishing or chamfering.


      Step 3

      Step 2


  4. Press to start the cycle.


    Once the cycle has been finished, remove the lens and wait 5 minutes before measuring it: you will obtain incorrect dimensions if the lens is warm and dilated.

  5. Precisely measure the 4 dimensions with a slide caliper.


    Step 5


  6. Go back to the setting screen for the drilling and shape axis position in technician mode.


    => > > >


  7. Input the values measured in the matching fields.


    Step 7


    The values taken into account are displayed on the right side of the screen:

    Adjustment calculations must remain in the tolerance range:

    • ± 2° on the axis position,

    • ± 2 mm on the Y adjustment.

      => never validate adjustments that are outside the tolerance range.

      Drill axis:

      axis position compensation.


      X Offset:

      adjustment of the horizontal position (not taken into account at this time) Y Offset:

      adjustment of the vertical position


  8. Once the modification has been made, press to return to the working screen and save the data.


The compensation values are not taken into account when making an after-sales shape.

The characteristics of the after-sales square made after any modification to the values will remain identical.
